《ambiences》

I began recording things during the pandemic after I started micing up my guqin for online lessons with a borrowed microphone. The first thing I noticed was how much more accurately I could hear the slide notes with my guqin. The second thing I noticed was how loud the sounds of my shared apartment were.

Eventually, I began to take recordings outside to capture the unique ambiences of the surrounding areas. The early pandemic completely transformed the soundscape of my neighborhood, and I began to appreciate the significance of formerly mundane sounds like the bustle of my street.

I continue to explore the everyday noises that mark the distinct character of a place. I categorize and number each of my recordings, leaving room for the possibility of recapturing that location in the future.
