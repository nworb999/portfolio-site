    • 《eastern parkway meditations》

    eastern parkway meditations (2021 - 2023) was a field recording project involving recordings taken in my apartment or along Eastern Parkway in Brooklyn. It began after moving into my apartment and noticing the faint rumble of the subway turning a corner beneath my street. Each of the tracks features the white noise inherent to my block, including the building vent on my balcony, the vacuum cleaner in my room as I clean, and the parkway outside.
