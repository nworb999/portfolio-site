《music to languish to》

music to languish to (2022 - present) is a soundtrack to do nothing to. More specifically, it is an ongoing project where I upload loops from my eurorack that evoke similar feelings of aimless relaxation. I source tracks that fit the criteria for this project while listening back to live jams.

I'm often working while listening to music, and in order to focus, I listen to repetitive music. I like the idea of music to do things to, and music often has the utilitarian task of motivating me throughout different parts of my day. Inspired by the rise of social movements such as 躺平 (tǎng píng), or "lying flat", I decided to focus on feelings of inactivity.
