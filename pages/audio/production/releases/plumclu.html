    • 《Plum , clu》

    Plum , clu (2023) is a compilation album (and record label) curated and founded by my talented friend, Paria Almasi.

    (nectar of sonic wonder...)

    "This release of sound is made by non-binary, trans, and female artists And anoints a ritual through its very release, the fruit-stone of which is a queer cross-section of audio: not boxed nor defined, it explores sonic craft in electronic form, outside convention in genre or being. It oozes out from fixtures and creates an irreducible essence, albeit from disparate strands of voice and identity. The scratch and soar inherent to Plum , clu demarcates a portal of sound-waves that carries you somewhere beyond time, while its practice in collection creation as archaic as prehistory. It is a celebration in itself, it is free to exist. Each track is intimate in coalescing one’ senses, extraterrestrial in its notes. Not a prototype, but living and becoming in its release as it meets the listener. ☂︎ ..."
