    • 《music technology》

    flowing water (2022 - present) is a work-in-progress musical interface inspired by the famous guqin tune 流水, or "Flowing Water". In modern renditions of the song, there is a section comprised entirely of 滾拂, or glissando, reminiscent of water flowing through a rocky stream.

    The synth is essentially an arpeggiator that emulates the texture of 滾拂. The chord is controlled by the X, Y position of a rock in a stream, which will emulate the press/slide notes of a finger by modifying the pitch of a single or multiple notes in the chord. Modeled on the standard tuning of the guqin, the app quantizes the browser window into seven different quadrants associated with the open string note of each guqin string on the X-axis, along with a continuous range of frequencies along the Y-axis of each quadrant to represent the sliding of the finger along the string. Using the Web Audio API, the arpeggiator will play and update with every drag of the rock across the screen.

      release plan

    • v0.0 is the current version, where the rock controls the pitch based on its position on the screen's X-axis, quantized to the frequencies of the seven open string notes.

    • v1.0 will introduce the arpeggiator and a basic background.
